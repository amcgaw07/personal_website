About Me

I started out my college career as a chemical engineer, but quickly realized I did not enjoy chemistry nearly as much as I thought (a somewhat major part of chemical engineering).

Chemistry != Fun

Thankfully the engineering program introduced me to programming, starting with basic JavaScript and eventually intermediate MATLAB and C++.

At one point when I was around 12 I tried to learn how to code due to exciting hacked Minecraft clients which people developed and I abused during the early stages of the game. I was shocked that someone could create such a robust computer program for fun. Despite this, my 12 year old brain could not handle the complex Java code I attempted to absorb through YouTube videos. I simply continued to use other peoples coded clients and my learning was unfortunately pushed to the side.

Fast forward 10 years later and the engineering program rekindled my interest in programming. I made the switch to computer science at the beginning of my Sophomore year and have been here since.
